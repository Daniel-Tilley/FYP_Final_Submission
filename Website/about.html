What is Training Pal?

Training Pal is an application that is intended to record information / data relating to training sessions in the form of a log. The application also offers functionality such as one to one video calls, video webinars and much more!


How does it differ from other apps?

Many of the existing solutions on the market are at times overcomplicated, clunky or can provide too much functionality. Training pal aims to keep things simple and also to make the experience very clean and intuitive for all users.


Why should I use training pal?

If you want to share data between you and your coach, if you want to use a simplistic and clean solution to manage your data, if you want to be a part of a new and exciting application, Training Pal is the app for you!


What will I gain from using training pal?

There is much to be gained from using the application. Its simplistic approach and clean user interface may lead to less headaches compared to using competitors products. Since Training Pal is a relatively young application, you can also expect much more functionality to be added down the line!
